Ragu Muthiah

Ragu starter his career in one the largest production houses in Chennai, armed with just an engineering degree and the passion to make the films.

Soon Ragu was in the thick of it, and a slew of films followed. Corporate films for Ford, Nike, Parryware, Saint Gobain and more.

In a short span, Ragu found himself heading the films division of JWT at Chennai.

Now it was time to plan and co ordinate the roll out of world-class commercials for various brands of the agency. Working in tandem with the creative teams, Ragu made headway to become a producer and a director who could put finely crafted commercials on screen.

His strength in the nuances of postproduction ensured that every film was delivered with the highest aesthetic quality.
