Saurabh Ghosh

Saurabh is the quintessential Bengali whose love for cinema led him to move into the city of Mumbai, to explore and soak in the myriad flavours of the medium. Working with top of the line directors who also made ad films, saw Saurabh’s portmanteau of work grow rapidly and showcase great variety.

Over the last 15 years, Saurabh’s work on Ericsson, Boroline, Hyundai and more have cut through the clutter while notching up awards at prestigious forums.
